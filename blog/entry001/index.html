THIS IS A DUMP FOR THE THINGS I DON'T KNOW WHERE TO PUT YET! I've always been a little funny about birthdays, and it's almost always been about the passage of time, and how little control I have over it. I always feel some sort of dread, some sense of loss, some deeply unsettling

Since I concluded that I couldn't in good faith count 'think of goals' as an actual goal within this 100 goal total, I decided that 'goal one' would be to not only think up the 99 others, but to build a website to house them all. That very website is the one you're visiting right now, and is a wonderfully broken and basic work in progress that has built upon a solid coding foundation that I developed when I was around ten years old and first discovered Neopets. I spend an awful lot of my life on the computer, and so learning to speak their language was a natural first port of call when considering what I'd like to achieve.

I'm hoping that this site will evolve as I do, and can grow from just a lowly bug-ridden goal dump and become a portolio of sorts, containing all the evidence of my personal growth and serving as a beacon of hope for anybody else who feels like time was leaving them behind.

In conclusion, hi, I'm Savannah (or Tinkerknock, as I'm known online), and I'm working on this site. And myself. In 100 different ways.
